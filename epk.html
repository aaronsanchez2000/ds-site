Short bio

Danny Styles is a Las Vegas producer and multi-instrumentalist making sample-based beat tapes, remixes, and full-band pop punk/rock music for fans of Cookin' Soul, Madvillain, and Hot Mulligan.

Long bio

Danny Styles is a producer and multi-instrumentalist from Las Vegas, Nevada, making beat tapes, remixes, and full-band pop punk/rock music.

His recent album Slappywax is a beat tape that moves through soft lofi loops, Dilla-inspired drums, distorted alien-sounding beats, and old-school boom bap. Under the Covers shows the other side of his music, with pop punk and rock covers played through his and Lo-Shi's own style. Drunk Dogs leans further into sample-based production, with loose drums, chopped-up soul samples, and a rough-around-the-edges feel.

Danny's work is entirely self-produced, with each project adding another piece to his world: part beat tape, part band project, part remix archive.

Listen to Slappywax, out everywhere, and follow Danny on Instagram and Spotify.
