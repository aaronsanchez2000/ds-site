About

Bio

Danny Styles is a producer and multi-instrumentalist from Las Vegas, Nevada, making sample-based beat tapes, remixes, and full-band pop punk/rock music.

His sound shifts between dusty, chopped-up production and loud, guitar-driven songs. Some releases lean into lofi loops, drum breaks, warped samples, and old-school boom bap, while others pull from pop punk and alternative rock with distorted guitars, live instruments, and full-band arrangements.

Everything is self-produced, from the beats and samples to the instruments, recording, mixes, and masters. Each project adds another piece to his world, whether it comes out as a beat tape, a remix, a cover, or some mixture of all of it.

Follow Danny on Instagram for new releases and updates, and listen on Spotify, Apple Music, or your preferred streaming service.
